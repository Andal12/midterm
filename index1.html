Children fiction is geared towards readers aged 12 to 18, although it has a broad readership. YA books often feature protagonists in the same age range, addressing themes relevant to adolescents while spanning various genres.

Sci-fi, or science fiction, is a genre that explores speculative concepts often related to futuristic technology, space exploration, time travel, and scientific advancements. It can be written for any age group, blending imaginative elements with scientific or futuristic concepts.

Adult fiction contains literature written for and marketed to Adult, encompassing a wide range of genres and themes. It caters to a mature audience and often explores complex topics.
